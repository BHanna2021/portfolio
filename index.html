The Legend, the Mystery, the Truth

Did I get your attention? I hope so and further hope you're willing to learn a little about me and what I have to offer.

Legend has it that I didn't sweat school as learning came naturally and easily - too easily, and boredom often set in while stuck in class listening to the same explanations on repeat.
This, the desire for real-world experience and freedom, plus the realization that I didn't know what I wanted to be when I grew up drove me to avoid pursuit of higher education.
I still have no desire for heavy student loans, nor do I wish to subject myself to the hours and hours of basic courses that would be required in pursuit of any degree.
Having said that, would you believe that I live to learn?

The mystery of my life for many people, is how deeply my love of learning affects and drives me.
I'm the first to tell you that you start to lose me once you start limiting my reach, my training, my ability to grow and yet I have surprised many.
Think of the air we must breathe to live and understand how strong the pull of learning is to me.

The truth is after over a decade spent in the finance arena, working various lower level positions, I realized that it just isn't my passion.
What I have come to understand is that I am driven not only to learn but to find ways to make things better for others. Whether I am creating documents to help others learn how to work through
various processes, training others on how applications work so it takes some of the frustration and stress out of their day, or simply sharing the knowledge I have gained over my years in business -
I am happiest when I am able to be helpful and continue to learn.

I am excited that I took the opportunity to join the IT world - it opens up direct access to limitless learning and opportunites to make things better for all kinds of people.
I am forever grateful to my co-worker for telling me about the Web Development part-time bootcamp with Eleven Fifty Academy - thank you Angie!
My creativity has come out of hiding and I look forward to each challenge and assignment so I can learn more.


In closing, I bring passion, drive, and creativity to whatever I take on - challenge me and you'll see...
